TY Work Experience at Red Hat
November 14th-25th 2022

Day 1

@caoimheoregan

On the first day we started at 10am, we had an introduction and got to learn about the types of things that Red Hat do. I found this very interesting as I didnt know much about them before hand. We learned that they are an open-source software company. Throughout the day we took part in many workshops. we had a workshop with Jess where we learned about using an app called inkscape to design our own logos for the office games room, first we drew our ideas on paper and then we started to work on them using InkScape. After lunch we had a workshop with Fiona and Tomas where we learned how to code using GitHub, at first I found it queit difficult but after a while it became a lot easier to understand. I really enjoyed my first day of work experience at Red Hat as it was great fun and I got to meet new people, make new friends and learn new things. .

Day 2

@caoimheoregan

We started working at 9:30am where we were taught some more about vs code, we then started to edit our own blogs which we will be updating each morning throughout the week. next we had a workshop with Owen where we learned about agile. we used a website called trello to create our own to do, doing and done lists which will help us keep track of the different activitie we will be doing throughout the week. I enjoyed learning about this method of working and I think it will be very useful in the future. Next we had a talk from David about his career journey and how he got to where hs is now. We then had lunch where we got to chat to eachother and play in the games room. After lunch we had a Microbit workshop with Dominik which i really enjoyed as I have used microbit before so it was easy to keep up with Dominik as he was explaining. during this workshop we learned how to create a dice aswell as a daylight sensor and many other interesting things. . .
